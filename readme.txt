This plugin will add an image/logo of your choice to all of your RSS feeds.

== Description ==

This plugin will allow you to add one image or logo of your choice (defined in settings)
to your RSS feed header. It is very simple to install/maintain and will add your website's
presence to your RSS feeds by giving it this personal touch.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `rss-image-in-head` folder to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through your `Plugins` menu in WordPress.
3. Under `Settings`, visit `RSS Header Image` to set the URL for the logo/image you wish to display.
